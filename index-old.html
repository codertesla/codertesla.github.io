TALL NECK TRIBE

The Tall Neck Tribe (TNT) is a collection of 6,666 unique ERC-721 tokens stored on the Ethereum Blockchain.

We have a professional artist team with more than 17 artists and they have worked for almost 2 months to deliver 520 hand-crafted trait images.

The total possible combinations are 6,014,724,419,280 but only 6,666 of them were chosen by the algorithm which gives great uniqueness to each piece of the TNT artwork.

It has been 2100 years since the mysterious Tall Neck Tribe was discovered by humans on Earth.

Recently, the Chieftain has designed an access system driven by Ethereum smart contract. When you mint a TNT, you automatically get an exclusive NFT PASS CARD with access to the tribe.
